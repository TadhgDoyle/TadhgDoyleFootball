Tadhg Doyle • Player Profile 2025 • Background

Born on 28th February 2012 in Portiuncula University Hospital, Ballinasloe, Ireland. Tadhg has been raised in 'Carrownure Lower', a townland near the small village of Lecarrow in South Roscommon. Obsessed with football since he could walk, Tadhg's stated aim in life, since he could express himself, is to become a professional footballer. This remains his vision for himself and his ultimate goal. During his early years Tadhg played with the local 'St. John's Athletic' team. Following the relaxation of covid restrictions, it was decided to give Tadhg the opportunity to play his football in Dublin within the DDSL league.

Tadhg Doyle • Player Profile 2025 • Overview

Tadhg is an incredibly skilled, gifted, aware, technical midfielder. He moulds himself in the impression of a combination of players such as Scholes, Iniesta, Verrati and Modric. Genuinely, and naturally two footed (constant work on what might be considered his weaker side, has often led to confusion as to which foot he actually plays off). His engine is well attested, measured and acknowledged, making him a true box to box player. Tadhg's preferred role is1 that of a '6', effectively 'quarterbacking' the game, ensuring efficient transition between the thirds. An extremely versatile player, Tadhg can perform and excel in any midfield role, play wing back on either side, play 8,10 or false 9.
